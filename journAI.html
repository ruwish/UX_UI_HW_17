Project Summary
JournAI is an innovative journaling application designed to revolutionize the way individuals reflect, express, and grow through the power of artificial intelligence (AI). Our app aims to utilize cutting-edge AI technologies to enhance the user experience by providing personalized prompts, analyzing trends and patterns in personal entries, and ensuring top-notch security and privacy.  At times, it’s beneficial to take a step back and introspect. JournAI enables individuals to delve into profound insights about their thoughts and emotions, nurturing self-reflection and personal growth within a safe and intelligent digital setting.

What Are We Trying to Achieve?
The primary goal of JournAI is to break down barriers to journaling, enabling users to gain deeper insights into their thoughts and emotions. Secondary goals encompass promoting regular journaling, enhancing mindfulness and emotional intelligence, providing community interactions, and more. We want to take journaling to the next level and have this app become a tool that everyone can use.
Design Principle
5 user interviews were conducted in order for us to gain insights into what users value in a journaling app and their thoughts on journaling.

Key takeaways from the insights:
  • Users want to gain structure and goal setting benefits from journaling
  • Users want to write out feelings to help them articulate and understand themselves better
  • Use it to keep track of things that happen in dailies lives or simply a to-do list

Interface Design
We embraced the concept of familiarity as a guiding principle in designing the overall layout. Drawing inspiration from the intuitive designs of applications like Notes (iOS) and Gmail, we adopted a sidebar approach to organize data and activities. This strategic decision allows users to effortlessly access their past entries, enhancing the overall user experience.
Many individuals often struggle to initiate their journaling journey due to a lack of ideas or topics. Thus, one of our key focal points is integrating AI-powered enhancements to provide customized prompts to give a more personalized experience. This feature aims to alleviate this hurdle. However, for those who prefer an open canvas for their thoughts, a blank template option will also be available to cater to their specific needs.
Incorporating AI Into the Picture
By leveraging AI-driven personalization, the app offers tailored prompts, address a common struggle where users are unable to come up with ideas. Integrating AI analysis allow users to easily track their emotional fluctuations over time, thereby fostering self-reflection and personal growth.
By toggling on different options, users are able utilize different AI enhanced features. They also have the option to deactivate AI as a whole if they don’t feel comfortable using it.
The recommendation option here tailor prompts and topics to users, inspiring meaningful and enriching reflections.
Closing Thoughts
Our case study has shed light on the incredible potential of AI enhanced journaling apps in the realm of personal growth and self reflection. By incorporating AI and UX principles, we aim to empower users to embark on a journey of self-discovery and emotional well-being.
To elevate this project, we envision implementing community interactions, insightful blog posts, and an abundant of valuable resources as the next step. The ultimate goal is to transform JournAI into a daily companion, offering users an enriching and dependable tool for their everyday needs.
